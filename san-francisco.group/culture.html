Culture

We’re building an environment that is both technically and creatively inspiring. From tech-talks to artist-in-residence workshops, our vision is to educate through participation.

As part of the Rackspace San Francisco office, we’re able to utilize direct connections to industry leaders who have experience building great companies. We also host contributors and specialists in: node.js, twisted python, cassandra, openstack, and many more open-source projects.

And if your mind desires to escape beyond just product, there will be unique activities for members. From learning how to tune a bike to volunteering in the community to karaoke sessions. Plus we have flavor, our kitchen is stocked with delicious snacks and Sriracha sauces. We even have two turntables and a microphone.

And of course, we also host many wonderful events.
