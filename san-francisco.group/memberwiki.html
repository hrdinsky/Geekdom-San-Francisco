What do I do if I’ve lost my badge or I think I’ve lost my badge?

Report a lost badge immediately, even if you aren’t sure or think that the badge “might be in my car.” We can shut down your badge temporarily and re-activate it when it is found. Report a lost badge to badgesf@geekdom.com. If you determine that your badge is permanently lost, there will be a $10 fee for replacement.

{dot_border}

What do I do if I forgot my badge at home?

We will issue you a temporary badge at the front desk. Sharing badges is not permissible. Your name is attached to your badge and you are responsible for any access given with your badge.

{dot_border}

What do I do if I have a guest?

Please let sf@geekdom.com know and he will print a visitor badge in advance.

All guests must check-in prior to entering the work area. Guests must be accompanied by you when in the work area.

{dot_border}

What do I do if I have an employee, contractor, or intern joining for a week or a day?

Please let sf@geekdom.com know and he will issue a temporary badge to your visiting team member. All visiting team members must sign an NDA and a Member Agreement.

{dot_border}

What do I do if I have visiting team members in excess of my desk capacity?

Work with sf@geekdom.com to make arrangements in advance. You can add extra community memberships for the required dates.

{dot_border}

Lockers & Cubbies

Limited availability combination lockers are accessible to Geekdom members for the fee of $10/month. Geekdom SF and Rackspace are not responsible for lost or stolen personal items.

{dot_border}

Locking File Cabinets

Locking File Cabinets are available on a first-come first-served basis to Premium Members. You will be issued one key for your cabinet. The fee for replacement of a lost key is $25.

{dot_border}

Something is broken, or I have IT problems

First, check the Geekdom Wiki to see if you can troubleshoot on your own. If you are not able to resolve your problem independently, then shoot an email to sf@geekdom.com

To give feedback, both positive and constructive, send an email to sf@geekdom.com

Defaulted or Delayed Membership Payments

Membership Fees are due on the 1st or 15th of the month, depending on your membership cycle. If your payment is more than 5 days late, your badge(s) may be de-activated. Badge(s) will not be reactivated until payment is received.

Kitchen & Pantry

The Kitchen is open to all Members! Please help yourself to the food and beverage included with your membership. One refrigerator is reserved for shared food, and the other is meant for individual food. Please label your individual food with name and date. The individual food refrigerator will be cleaned every Friday--items left over the weekend will be discarded.

Emergency & First Aid

What to do in an Emergency? Call 911 and move to safety.

To contact the Local Police for a non-emergency, dial 415-553-0123 or 415-553-1373.

To contact the Local Fire Department for a non-emergency, dial 415-558-3200.

First Aid supplies are located in the lobby near the restrooms, above the water fountain.

Culture at Geekdom San Francisco

One benefit of co-working at Geekdom is access to all public events hosted at Rackspace San Francisco. Geekdom members are welcome to participate in public Meet-Ups and Hackathons in the Racker Rally Room.

How do I print?

[SFO PRINTER on OSX]

Xerox ColorQube 9203 @ 10.20.68.10

  • Go to Print & Scan from System Preferences
  • Click the [+] button
  • Click the IP button
  • Enter 10.20.68.10 as the Address
  • Give it a sec to think and detect the printer
  • Change the Name of the printer to something more memorable if you’d like
  • Confirm the driver is set to Xerox ColorQube 9203 or Generic PostScript Printer and click Add
  • Accept the default configuration

I want to host an event in the RRR. How do I book the room?

Send an email to sf@geekdom.com with your meeting request. Include the following information: Name of event, hosts and sponsors, dates and times, number of anticipated attendees, any A/V requirements, name of catering company or other event vendors, your contact information. Your request will be approved or denied within one week of request, depending on the availability of the room.

{dot_border}

How often and for how long can I book a Conference Room?

Premium Members get 8 hours/week and Community Members get 2 hours/week. Conference Rooms are bookable by the hour and half hour by emailing sf@geekdom.com. Conference Rooms are each equipped with a large display and AppleTV for easy streaming. Breakout rooms and Phone Booths are available on a first-come-first-served basis and are “unlimited” in their use.

{dot_border}

How often and for how long can I book the event venue - Racker Rally Room (RRR)?

The RRR is a shared resource. It can be booked for hackathons, special events, meetups, and the like. It has a capacity of 65 seated guests and 100 standing. The sliding door can be opened to accommodate larger events. Companies with Premium Membership may use the RRR for up to 8 hours/month and companies with Community Membership may use the RRR for up to 3 hours/month. Reservations will be accepted based on availability. Use of the RRR requires sponsorship by a Racker.

There is a special application process for use of the RRR in excess of the guidelines above. Please submit an application here.

{dot_border}

Can I host a Board Meeting in “The Alamo”?

Yes, you can! Much like the RRR, it is a shared resource. The Alamo accommodates up to 20 seated guests. Bookings will be prioritized based on availability. The use of The Alamo will be considered in your total hours/month of Conference Room bookings. Please email sf@geekdom.com to make a booking request. Use of The Alamo requires sponsorship by a Racker.

{dot_border}

How do I get on the secure wireless network R-Geek?

You will be given a shared access code. Please use a VPN for security.

Phones and Phone Booths

Dial 9 to reach an outside line. To dial long distance or international numbers, you will need a phone calling card. The phone booths are not reservable--they are available on a first come, first served basis.
