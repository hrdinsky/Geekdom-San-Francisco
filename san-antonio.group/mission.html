Our Mission

Like a gym membership for geeks: Our mission is to provide an inspiring environment for bright minds to explore ideas in entrepreneurship, technology, leadership, and creativity, develop talents and connect to a community of like-minded people.

Five Pillars of Geekdom

Geekdom is built on five immutable principles that have never been available in one place before.

The Power of

Open

The power of Open. We admit high-schoolers, undergrad students, startups, corporate fugitives, basement inventors, young entrepreneurs, established small businesses, etc—All on their merit, their ideas, and their passion for entrepreneurship and technology.

The Power of

Mentors

The power of Mentors. Teaching, training and workshops are a deep part of the daily fabric. Part of our membership is paying with your 'time'. You must give back 1 hour a week to work with individuals & teams or give a workshop once a month to be a member. This ensures collaboration because the resources of other like-minded people are there to guide and teach when you need it.

The Power of

Membership

The power of Membership. Geekdom is very low-cost to admitted members. Geekdom is about building technologists and entrepreneurs with the power of collaboration. Geekdom is not just about renting desks. This is our difference.

The Power of

Heart-Set

The power of the Heart-Set. Geekdom promotes and trains the entrepreneurial Heart-set as much as the mind-set: permission to fail and the real role of risk as an opportunity. This cannot be taught at Harvard or any university to date. It has to happen in practice. No successful entrepreneur hasn’t failed numerous times. We believe passion guides developers and startups and they must work through what doesn’t work short term to be successful long term.

The Power of

Mission

The power of Mission. Geekdom is mission based, like any great enterprise. You belong at Geekdom if you share our belief that entrepreneurship is a vehicle of community progress and human progress, not just financial progress. We are a force for good.

San Antonio is a top national and regional hub for attracting, retaining, engaging, and growing entrepreneurial, technology, and creative talent. Sponsoring corporations and members participate and build a vibrant community which stimulates the development of the entire at Geekdom."
